AcousticComfortc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://www.ibp.fraunhofer.de/ontologies/hex#AcousticComfort

A concept of IEQ factor representing acoustic comfort. It can be described by the combination of quality and acoustic sensation.
is equivalent to
IEQFactor c and (hasAuditorySensation op some AuditorySensation c)
has super-classes
IEQFactor c
has sub-classes
LoudAcousticComfort c, QuietAcousticComfort c
is in domain of
hasAuditorySensation op
